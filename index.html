Bio

I am Amirmohammad Mohseni Ghaleh Ghazi, a try to be creative mind deeply rooted in artistry and technological innovation person.
Originating from Iran, I embarked on a transformative journey to Italy and Torino to harness my passion and seize the abundant opportunities available.
Driven by a fascination for software and data analysis, I currently thrive as a Project Manager at Artudio in Tehran, Iran. Here, I intricately blend graphic design, illustration, and CSS into cohesive projects, reflecting my dedication to merging art and technology.
Additionally, my journey includes enriching experiences in the hospitality industry due to the fact that I worked as a waiter and a salesman for for than 1 year in Italy, honing skills in customer service and order management. These experiences, coupled with pursuing a Bachelor's in Computer Engineering at Politecnico Di Torino, have deepened my digital proficiency.
My aspirations are anchored in the world of data and software, and Italy provides the perfect canvas to bring these dreams to fruition. I am committed to leveraging these passions, forging a path that seamlessly integrates creativity, data analysis, and software expertise.
